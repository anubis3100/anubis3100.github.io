# welcome

this is the first post on the blog. anything written here will show up on the site.

the title of the post comes from the filename — so `welcome.md` becomes "welcome".

you can write in **bold**, *italic*, or [link out](https://twitter.com/anubis_3100). lists work too:

- thought one
- thought two
- thought three

and quotes:

> we are nothing but mere servants who sit underneath the primordial seat of being.

new posts: just drop a new `.md` file into the `blog/` folder on github and it'll appear here automatically.
